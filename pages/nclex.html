NCLEX Preparation Guide

How to Prepare for the NCLEX

The NCLEX (National Council Licensure Examination) is the exam required for nurses who want to practice in the United States and Canada. Proper preparation significantly increases the chances of passing on the first attempt.

NCLEX 8-Week Study Plan

Recommended Study Strategy

  • Study consistently every day.
  • Focus on fundamentals and patient safety.
  • Use NCLEX practice question banks.
  • Review pharmacology and medical surgical nursing.
  • Take multiple practice exams before the test.
