Nursing Career FAQ

Common questions about working abroad as a nurse.

Many countries require IELTS or another English test. However, some US states may waive the requirement if your nursing education was in English.

International nurses must pass the NCLEX-RN exam to obtain nursing licensure in the United States.

The USA, Canada, the UK, and Australia frequently recruit international nurses due to shortages.

The process usually takes between 1 and 3 years depending on exams, credential evaluation, and visa processing.
