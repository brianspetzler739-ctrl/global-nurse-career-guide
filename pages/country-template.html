Work as a Nurse Abroad

Complete step-by-step career pathway for international nurses.

Step 1 — Check Eligibility

Verify that your nursing degree is recognized in the destination country.

Step 2 — Credential Evaluation

Many countries require evaluation of your education and training.

Step 3 — English Language Exam

You may need IELTS or another English test.

Step 4 — Licensing Examination

Examples include NCLEX, CBT, or other national exams.

Step 5 — Apply for Jobs

Search for hospitals and recruitment agencies that sponsor international nurses.

Step 6 — Visa Application

Apply for the appropriate work visa once employment is secured.
